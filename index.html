About

I am a Robotics Engineering student at the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) with a strong passion for building practical, reliable, and intelligent robotic systems. I thrive in technical environments where problem solving, iteration, and teamwork are central, and I am motivated by turning ideas into functional prototypes and real world solutions.

I am always curious, always learning, and always excited to take on new engineering challenges.
